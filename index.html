About Mechichs

Mechichs was born out of an ambitious university project aimed at making vehicle repair services more accessible to the everyday consumer. Initially launched by a group of passionate students at LPU University, our goal was simple: to connect people with trustworthy mechanics and garage services in the most efficient way possible.

We recognized the challenge faced by vehicle owners when searching for reliable service providers. Hence, we developed a platform that bridges the gap between customers and garages, offering seamless appointment bookings, services, and real-time availability updates.

What started as a small university project has now grown into a leading solution in the vehicle service industry, trusted by numerous customers and partnered with many skilled garages. We're constantly innovating and adding new features to make vehicle maintenance easier for everyone.
